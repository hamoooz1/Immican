The problem immigrants face

Immigrating to Canada is complex and the multitude of available services can be overwhelming. Deciding where to start is one of the biggest challenges facing Immigrants today.

Culture shock and a confusing system lead to mismatches between the services sought and services required.

Language proficiency, cultural adaptation, and the recognition of foreign credentials can contribute to the time it takes for immigrants to fully integrate into the Canadian workforce and community.
